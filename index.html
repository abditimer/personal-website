Abdi Timer

Advisory Data Scientist at IBM

Abdi Timer is an Advisory Data Scientist at IBM, where he leads the delivery of Generative AI solutions to customers across the UK and Ireland, managing C-level relationships and architectural decisions for enterprise AI implementations.

Previously, Abdi worked as a Senior Data Scientist at Legal & General, where he developed machine learning models for competitor pricing deployed on Azure. He also served as a Senior Data Scientist at Capco, creating bespoke data models that reduced legacy solution deployment times from 12+ hours to under 1 minute per run.

Abdi is currently pursuing his PhD in AI at the University of Birmingham, and serves as Lead AI Instructor at BrainStation, where he advances AI education and helps organizations solve complex problems with cutting-edge solutions.
