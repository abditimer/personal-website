About

AI researcher and educator with enterprise delivery roots.

Abdi Timer is an AI researcher, educator, and practitioner based in London. He currently works at NVIDIA as a Technical AI Instructor across EMEA, helping partners understand, build, and deploy AI in practice.

Alongside industry work, he is pursuing a PhD in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Birmingham. His research combines mechanistic interpretability and AI safety to understand how large language models work internally.

Before NVIDIA, he spent more than four years at IBM advising enterprise clients on AI and machine learning, taught AI at BrainStation, and earlier launched an HR chatbot used by 7,000 Legal & General employees.
